Who we are

we battle generational trauma

National Youth Commission

A government agency in the Philippines that specifically addresses issues surrounding the Filipino youth. They are the voice and advocate of the youth. The National Youth Commission is a government agency tasked with promoting the interests and welfare of young Filipinos.

Philippine Youth Leadership Program

PYLP is built to develop the leadership skills of young Filipinos and empower them to become agents of change in their communities. The PYLP is to enhance participants’ knowledge, skills, leadership abilities, and confidence so that they can engage the world around them.

Kabataan Partylist

Kabataan Partylist is a youth organization that represents the interests of young Filipinos in the Philippine House of Representatives. The partylist represents the youth sector, and aims to "galvanize the Filipino youth’s unity for social change."

YOUTH EMPOWERED

An organization built to battle generational trauma and its effects. The organization promotes healing and resilience, the establishment of a support network for individuals and families impacted by generational trauma, and improved mental and physical health outcomes.
